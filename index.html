Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | 1931 - 2015

About Him

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India and a world-renowned scientist. He was admired for his simplicity, knowledge, and contribution to India’s space and missile programs. Apart from being a great leader, he inspired millions of young students to dream big.

Key Achievements

  • Led India’s Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Served as the 11th President of India (2002-2007).
  • Wrote inspiring books like Wings of Fire and Ignited Minds.
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor.
